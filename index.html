Welcome to the Rent Company

  • We are a Mumbai based start-up and our goal is to create a rental model for students, to make their needs accessible and affordable.

  • We are working on a platform where we help in connecting you to the fellow renters from your college/university campus.

  • You can put up requests for anything on our website as per your need. You can rent out things sitting at your homes to your fellow students in college/university campus to earn a few bucks on the side while studying.
